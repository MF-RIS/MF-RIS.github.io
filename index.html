MF-RIS: A General RIS Architecture

Deploying Multi-Functional RIS (MF-RIS), enjoying signal reflection, refraction, and amplification simultaneously!

Advantage

Achieves full-space coverage
Mitigate the double-fading issue
Enjoy better performance with smaller element

Motivation

The single-side coverage and double-fading attenuation restrict the practical application of conventional RIS. So, we propose a novel concept of MF-RIS. Providing reflection, refraction, and amplification simultaneously for the incident signal, MF-RIS will enhance the performance of communication.
